Note that phenotype pairs is n1*n2 heterogenous GWAS studies, where n1 is the number of phenotypes in GWAS 1,
n2 is the number of phenotypes in GWAS 2 (both after p-threshold filtering)

Phenotype pairs for homogenous GWAS studies is n1*n1/2 (number of combinations)

Phenotype pairs for GWAS1 with joint GWAS2, GWAS3 is n1*n2*n3

Note that these may be adjusted by limiting phenotype pairs that have a no-null SNP intersection
